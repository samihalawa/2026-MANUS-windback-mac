WindBack is a privacy-focused macOS application that helps you remember everything you've seen on your screen.

KEY FEATURES:

• Screen Recording & OCR
Continuously captures your screen activity and extracts text using advanced OCR technology, making everything searchable.

• AI-Powered Search with RAG
Uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation to intelligently search through your screen history. Ask natural questions like "What was I working on yesterday?" and get accurate answers.

• Clipboard History
Never lose copied content again. WindBack tracks your clipboard history including text, images, URLs, and files.

• Timeline Navigation
Scroll through your digital history with an intuitive timeline view. Jump to any moment in your past activity.

• Multi-Cascade Context
Advanced RAG system organizes your history into immediate (4h), historical (7d), and thematic (all-time) context tiers for optimal AI understanding.

• Privacy First
All data stays on your device. No cloud uploads, no tracking, no telemetry. All AI processing happens locally or with your own API keys.

• Gemini AI Integration
Connect your Google Gemini API key for powerful AI-assisted analysis of your screen history.

PERFECT FOR:
• Developers who need to recall code snippets
• Researchers tracking sources and references
• Professionals who attend many meetings
• Anyone who wants a better digital memory
